Multi-Beam Sonar Systems are equipped with multiple transducers that emit and receive sonar signals in a wider area, providing high-resolution 3D images of the underwater environment. Side Scan Sonar Systems emit a fan-shaped beam to the sides of the vessel, producing detailed images of the sea floor or underwater objects. Both technologies play a crucial role in various industries such as marine exploration, fisheries, and defense.</p></p>
