<p><p>Ammonia electric heaters are available in two main types: Hairpin type and Solenoid type. Hairpin type heaters consist of a coiled heating element that heats the ammonia directly. Solenoid type heaters use a solenoid coil to generate heat by passing an electric current through the ammonia. Both types of heaters are used in industrial and commercial applications where precise temperature control is required. Hairpin type heaters are often chosen for their efficiency and compact size, while solenoid type heaters are preferred for their fast heating ability.</p></p>
