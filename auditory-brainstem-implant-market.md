The Decoding Chip processes these signals, translating them into stimuli for the brainstem. The Electrodes then deliver these stimuli to the auditory nerve, allowing for sound perception. Each component plays a crucial role in enabling individuals with hearing impairments to receive auditory input and improve their overall quality of life.</p></p>
