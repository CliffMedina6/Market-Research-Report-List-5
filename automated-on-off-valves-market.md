<p><p>Automated on-off valves are classified into different types based on their design and functionality. Angle valves are commonly used in tight spaces, ball valves offer quick shut-off with a quarter-turn operation, control valves regulate the flow of liquids or gases, float valves control the level of a liquid in a tank. Other types of automated on-off valves include butterfly valves, gate valves, and globe valves. Each type serves a specific purpose and is selected based on the application requirements.</p></p>
