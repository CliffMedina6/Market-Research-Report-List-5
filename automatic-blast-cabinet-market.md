<p><ul><li>Pressure Blast Cabinet</li><li>Suction Blast Cabinet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic Blast Cabinet Market includes two main types: Pressure Blast Cabinet and Suction Blast Cabinet. Pressure Blast Cabinet utilizes compressed air to propel abrasive material at high velocity, making it suitable for heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, Suction Blast Cabinet uses suction generated by a vacuum pump to transport abrasive material, offering more control and precision for delicate workpieces. Both types have their unique advantages and are commonly used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing for surface preparation and cleaning purposes.</p></p>
