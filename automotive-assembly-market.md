<p><p>In the automotive assembly market, there are two main types: full-automatic and semi-automatic. Full-automatic assembly lines involve machinery and robots carrying out the majority of tasks with minimal human intervention. This type of assembly is highly efficient and precise, making it suitable for high-volume production. Semi-automatic assembly lines, on the other hand, combine machinery with manual labor. This type of assembly is more versatile and allows for customization but may have lower production rates compared to full-automatic assembly lines.</p></p>
