<p><p>Automotive Continuously Variable Transmission System is categorized into Belt-Chain Drive CVT and Planetary CVT types. Belt-Chain Drive CVT utilizes a belt or chain to transfer power between two pulleys, offering smooth acceleration and fuel efficiency. On the other hand, Planetary CVT uses a system of gears and pulleys to provide a wide range of gear ratios, improving overall performance and control. Both types have their unique features and benefits, catering to different preferences and demands in the automotive market.</p></p>
