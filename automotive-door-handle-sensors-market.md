<p><p>Automotive door handle sensors can be categorized into two types: Button Type and Induction Type. Button Type sensors require physical pressure to be applied to open the door, typically through a push button mechanism. On the other hand, Induction Type sensors use proximity sensors to detect the presence of a hand or object near the door handle, triggering it to open automatically. Both types offer convenience and ease of use, catering to different preferences and needs in the automotive market.</p></p>
