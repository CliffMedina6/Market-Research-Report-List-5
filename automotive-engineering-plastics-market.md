<p><p>Automotive engineering plastics market can be classified into two main types: thermosetting type and thermoplastics type. Thermosetting plastics are rigid materials that undergo a chemical reaction during the molding process, becoming permanently hardened. They offer high durability and resistance to heat and chemicals. On the other hand, thermoplastics are more flexible and can be molded and remolded multiple times without undergoing a chemical change. They are known for their impact resistance and recyclability, making them a popular choice in the automotive industry.</p></p>
