<p><p>The automotive front windscreen market offers two main types of glass - laminated glass and tempered glass. Laminated glass is made by sandwiching a layer of plastic between two layers of glass, providing increased safety and reducing the risk of shattering upon impact. Tempered glass, on the other hand, is subjected to a special heat treatment process, making it stronger than regular glass and less likely to break into sharp, dangerous shards. Both types offer unique benefits and cater to different consumer preferences in the automotive industry.</p></p>
