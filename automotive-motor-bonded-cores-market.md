<p><strong>The Automotive Motor Bonded Cores Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Rolled Non-Oriented (CRNO)</li><li>CRGO</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Motor Bonded Cores are used in the manufacturing of electric motors. Two common types of cores include Cold Rolled Non-Oriented (CRNO) and Cold Rolled Grain Oriented (CRGO). CRNO cores are made from non-oriented electrical steel, providing high magnetic permeability and low core loss. CRGO cores are made from grain-oriented electrical steel, offering improved magnetic properties and lower losses than CRNO cores. Both types play a crucial role in the efficiency and performance of automotive motors.</p></p>
