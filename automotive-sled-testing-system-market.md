<p><ul><li>Decelerator Sled Testing System</li><li>Catapult Sled Testing System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive sled testing systems are designed to simulate real-world crash scenarios for vehicle safety testing. Decelerator sled testing systems focus on slowing down a moving sled to simulate a crash deceleration, allowing for the study of occupant restraints and impact protection systems. On the other hand, Catapult sled testing systems propel a sled forward rapidly to simulate a frontal impact, providing valuable data on seat belt effectiveness and crashworthiness. Both types play a crucial role in evaluating and improving automotive safety measures.</p></p>
