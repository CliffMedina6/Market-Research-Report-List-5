<p><p>In the aviation refueling market, there are several types of refueling equipment available, including mobile tankers, hydrant dispensers, and fueling cabinets/skids. Mobile tankers are vehicles designed to transport fuel directly to aircraft for refueling. Hydrant dispensers are stationary units connected to underground hydrant systems at airports, allowing for efficient and rapid refueling. Fueling cabinets/skids are compact units that can be installed on smaller airports or for temporary refueling operations. Each type of equipment caters to different needs and requirements within the aviation refueling market.</p></p>
