<p><p>Bidirectional knife gate valves are available in two main types: wafer type and lug type. Wafer type knife gate valves are designed to fit between two flanges and are held in place by bolts. They are a more compact option and are ideal for applications where space is limited. Lug type knife gate valves have threaded inserts that allow them to be bolted directly into a system without the need for flanges. They provide a more secure and reliable connection, making them suitable for higher pressure applications.</p></p>
