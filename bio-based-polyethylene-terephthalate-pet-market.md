<p><p>Bio-based PET can be sourced from biomass, bio-based precursors, or bacteria. Biomass-derived PET is produced from plant-based sources, such as sugarcane or corn. Bio-based precursors, like bio-based ethylene glycol, are used to create PET through chemical processes. PET can also be produced from bacteria through fermentation. These different types of bio-based PET offer sustainable alternatives to traditional petroleum-based plastics, reducing reliance on fossil fuels and decreasing environmental impact.</p></p>
