<p><p>Bismaleimide (BMI) Prepreg comes in two main types: Unidirectional BMI Prepreg and Fabric BMI Prepreg. Unidirectional BMI Prepreg is composed of continuous fibers aligned in a single direction, providing high strength and stiffness in that direction. Fabric BMI Prepreg, on the other hand, consists of woven or stitched fibers in multiple directions, offering more balanced mechanical properties. Both types are widely used in aerospace, automotive, and electronics industries for their excellent thermal stability, high strength-to-weight ratio, and chemical resistance.</p></p>
