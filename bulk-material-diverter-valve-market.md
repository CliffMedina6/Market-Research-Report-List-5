<p><p>Bulk material diverter valves are commonly categorized into different types based on their design and functionality. Round flange diverter valves are characterized by a circular flange for easy installation and sealing. Square flange diverter valves have a square-shaped flange for a secure and stable connection. Gravity diverter valves rely on gravity to divert materials without the need for additional power. Other types of diverter valves may include slide gates, knife gates, and plug valves, each offering unique features and benefits for specific applications.</p></p>
