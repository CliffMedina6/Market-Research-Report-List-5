<p><p>The Carbon Dioxide Laser Market consists of three main types: Continuous Output, Pulse Output, and Q-Switch Output. Continuous Output lasers deliver a constant beam of energy over a sustained period of time, ideal for applications requiring steady power delivery. Pulse Output lasers emit short bursts of energy at intermittent intervals, beneficial for precision cutting and welding. Q-Switch Output lasers provide high-energy pulses in very short durations, suitable for precise material ablation and industrial processing applications.</p></p>
