<p><ul><li>Carbon-Composition Resistor</li><li>Carbon-Film Resistor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carbon resistor market can be broadly divided into two types: Carbon-Composition Resistor and Carbon-Film Resistor. Carbon-Composition Resistor is made by mixing carbon granules with a binding agent, offering high stability and low cost. On the other hand, Carbon-Film Resistor is made by depositing a layer of carbon film on a ceramic substrate, providing a more precise and stable resistance value. Both types are widely used in electronic circuits for their reliability and affordability.</p></p>
