<p><p>Cell line generation market includes various types such as knockout cell lines, overexpression cell lines, inducible expression cell lines, knockdown cell lines, knockin cell lines, and others. Knockout cell lines involve the deletion of specific genes, while overexpression cell lines involve the introduction of extra copies of genes. Inducible expression cell lines allow researchers to control gene expression, while knockdown cell lines reduce gene expression. Knockin cell lines involve the insertion of specific genes. Other types may include reporter cell lines or stable cell lines with specific modifications for research purposes.</p></p>
