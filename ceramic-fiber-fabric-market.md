<p><strong>The Ceramic Fiber Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ceramic Oxide Fiber</li><li>Ceramic Non-Oxide Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic fiber fabric market includes two main types based on the composition of the fibers: Ceramic Oxide Fiber and Ceramic Non-Oxide Fiber. Ceramic Oxide Fiber is made from oxides such as alumina and silica, offering high thermal stability and resistance to corrosion. On the other hand, Ceramic Non-Oxide Fiber is typically made from carbides and nitrides, providing excellent mechanical properties and high-temperature performance. Both types of ceramic fiber fabrics are widely used in various industries for insulation, fire protection, and composite materials.</p></p>
