<p><ul><li>Desi</li><li>Kabuli</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chickpea powders can be categorized into two main types - Desi and Kabuli. Desi chickpea powder is made from a smaller, darker variety of chickpeas, and is commonly used in Indian and Middle Eastern cuisines. It has a stronger, nuttier flavor compared to Kabuli chickpea powder. Kabuli chickpea powder, on the other hand, is made from a larger, lighter-colored variety of chickpeas. It is commonly used in Western cuisines and has a milder taste. Both types of chickpea powders have their own unique characteristics and uses in cooking.</p></p>
