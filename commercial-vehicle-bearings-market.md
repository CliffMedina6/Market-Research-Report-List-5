<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ball Bearings</li><li>Roller Bearings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial Vehicle Bearings Market includes two main types: Ball Bearings and Roller Bearings. Ball Bearings use balls to maintain separation between the races, reducing friction and allowing for smooth rotational movement. Roller Bearings use cylindrical rollers to distribute weight evenly across a larger surface area, enabling higher load capacities and greater precision. Both types are essential components in various commercial vehicles, ensuring smooth operation and durability in challenging conditions. The choice between ball and roller bearings depends on factors such as load capacity, speed, and application requirements.</p></p>
