<p><p>A Computer-assisted Dispatch System (CAD) is a software application designed to assist dispatchers in managing and coordinating emergency services such as police, fire, and ambulance services. CAD systems help dispatchers track the location of emergency vehicles, prioritize and assign calls, and maintain a record of incidents.</p><p>The Computer-assisted Dispatch System Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.1% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by factors such as increasing demand for advanced public safety solutions, rising call volumes for emergency services, and the need for efficient resource allocation in emergency response situations.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the CAD market is the integration of CAD systems with other public safety technologies such as Geographic Information Systems (GIS) and mobile data terminals. This integration allows for real-time data sharing between dispatchers, first responders, and other agencies, leading to improved situational awareness and faster response times.</p><p>Another trend driving the growth of the CAD market is the adoption of cloud-based CAD solutions by public safety agencies. Cloud-based CAD systems offer scalability, flexibility, and cost savings compared to traditional on-premises CAD systems, making them an attractive option for agencies looking to modernize their dispatch operations.</p></p>
