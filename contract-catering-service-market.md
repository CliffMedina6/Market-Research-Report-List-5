<p><ul><li>Fixed Price Contract</li><li>Cost Plus Contract</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fixed Price Contract is a type of contract catering service where the caterer charges a set price for their services regardless of the actual costs incurred. This provides stability and predictability for the client. On the other hand, Cost Plus Contract is a catering service where the caterer charges a price based on the actual costs incurred plus a markup for profit. This type of contract can be more flexible but may result in higher costs for the client.</p></p>
