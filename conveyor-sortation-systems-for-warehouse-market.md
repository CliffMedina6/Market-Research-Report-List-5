<p><p>Conveyor sortation systems for warehouse markets can be classified into two main types: linear sortation systems and loop sortation systems. Linear sortation systems involve products being sorted in a linear fashion along a conveyor belt, typically in a straight line. On the other hand, loop sortation systems involve products circulating in a loop on a conveyor belt, allowing for continuous sorting. Both systems are efficient solutions for warehouses looking to automate the sorting process and improve overall operational efficiency.</p></p>
