Aspiration catheters are used to remove blockages from blood vessels, while coronary balloon catheters inflate to open narrowed arteries. Diagnostic catheters help visualize the coronary arteries during procedures, and guide catheters assist in positioning other devices within the heart. These different types of catheters play a crucial role in the treatment of coronary artery disease and other cardiac conditions, providing options for physicians to effectively care for their patients.</p></p>
