<p><p>Curved glass market types include annealed curved glass, laminated curved glass, and double glazed curved glass. Annealed curved glass is a type of glass that has been heated and slowly cooled to improve its strength and durability. Laminated curved glass is made by sandwiching a layer of polyvinyl butyral between two sheets of curved glass, creating a strong and shatter-resistant material. Double glazed curved glass consists of two panes of curved glass separated by a layer of air or gas, providing enhanced insulation and noise reduction properties.</p></p>
