<p><p>Dairy-based ingredients market consists of two main types, Milk Ingredients and Whey Ingredients. Milk Ingredients include various products derived from milk such as milk powder, butter, cream, and cheese. These ingredients are widely used in the food and beverage industry for their flavor, texture, and nutritional benefits. Whey Ingredients, on the other hand, are derived from the liquid remaining after milk has been curdled and strained. Whey proteins, whey powder, and whey permeate are popular ingredients that are utilized in sports nutrition products and functional foods.</p></p>
