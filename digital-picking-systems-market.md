<p><p>Digital picking systems encompass both manual and auto-guided picking methods. Manual picking systems involve human operators physically selecting and fulfilling orders in a warehouse or distribution center. On the other hand, auto-guided picking systems utilize technology such as robots, drones, or automated guided vehicles to navigate the warehouse and retrieve items for order fulfillment. Both types of systems aim to make the picking process more efficient and accurate, but auto-guided systems tend to offer higher levels of automation and productivity.</p></p>
