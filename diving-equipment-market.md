<p><p>The diving equipment market can be categorized into three main types: recreational diving, clearance diving, and saturation diving. Recreational diving equipment is designed for casual divers who explore underwater environments for leisure and enjoyment. Clearance diving equipment is used for military and commercial purposes to locate and dispose of underwater explosives and hazards. Saturation diving equipment is utilized by professional divers working on offshore oil rigs or in underwater construction projects, allowing them to stay at depth for extended periods of time. Each market segment has specific equipment tailored to its requirements.</p></p>
