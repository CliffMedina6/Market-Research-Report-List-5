<p><p>DMLS 3D printers are categorized into two main types based on their powder feeding systems. Coaxial powder feeding systems involve the simultaneous feeding of both metal powder and gas into the printing chamber through a single nozzle. On the other hand, roller powder feeding systems utilize a roller mechanism to feed the metal powder into the chamber. Both systems play a crucial role in ensuring the precise and efficient printing of metal parts in the DMLS process.</p></p>
