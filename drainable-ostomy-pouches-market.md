<p><p>Drainable ostomy pouches are available in two main market types: one-piece bags and two-piece bags. One-piece bags are all-in-one systems where the pouch and skin barrier are combined into a single unit, making them convenient for quick changes. Two-piece bags have a separate skin barrier that attaches to the pouch, allowing for easier and more customized fitting. Both types offer drainable options for emptying waste, making them suitable for long-term ostomy care.</p></p>
