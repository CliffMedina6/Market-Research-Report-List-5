<p><p>Dry rotary vane pumps are categorized into two main types based on their mounting orientation - Horizontal Tank Mount and Vertical Tank Mount. Horizontal Tank Mount pumps are designed to be mounted horizontally on a tank or vessel, making them ideal for installations where space is limited. Vertical Tank Mount pumps, on the other hand, are designed to be mounted vertically on a tank or vessel, providing a more compact solution for applications with height restrictions.
