<p><p>The Electric Actuated Butterfly Valve market consists of two main types: Rubber Sealed and Metal Sealed. Rubber Sealed valves utilize elastomeric materials to create a tight seal when the valve is closed, making them suitable for applications where a flexible seal is needed. On the other hand, Metal Sealed valves use metal-to-metal contact to provide a more durable and long-lasting seal, making them ideal for high-pressure and high-temperature environments. Both types offer unique advantages and are chosen based on specific application requirements.</p></p>
