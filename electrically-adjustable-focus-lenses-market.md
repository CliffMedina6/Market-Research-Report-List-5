<p><strong>The Electrically Adjustable Focus Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrowetting-Based Liquid Lense</li><li>Liquid Crystal Lense</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrically adjustable focus lenses can be categorized into two main types: electrowetting-based liquid lenses and liquid crystal lenses. </p><p>Electrowetting-based liquid lenses use an applied electric field to alter the shape of a droplet of conductive liquid, allowing for precise control over the focal length of the lens. On the other hand, liquid crystal lenses rely on electrically induced changes in the orientation of liquid crystal molecules to achieve variable focus. Both types of electrically adjustable focus lenses offer rapid and accurate adjustments, making them ideal for applications such as medical imaging, photography, and augmented reality.</p></p>
