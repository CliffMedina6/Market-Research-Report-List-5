<p><p>Electricity trading platforms offer different types of markets for market participants to buy and sell electricity. Spot markets allow participants to trade electricity for immediate delivery at current market prices. This ensures real-time balance between electricity supply and demand. Futures markets, on the other hand, enable participants to agree on a price today for delivery of electricity at a future date. This helps in managing risks associated with fluctuating electricity prices and allows participants to hedge against price volatility.</p></p>
