<p><ul><li>Plywood</li><li>Oriented Strand Board (OSB)</li><li>Glued Laminated Timber (Glulam)</li><li>Laminated Veneer Lumber (LVL)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Engineered wood products like Plywood, Oriented Strand Board (OSB), Glued Laminated Timber (Glulam), and Laminated Veneer Lumber (LVL) are commonly used in construction due to their strength, durability, and versatility. Plywood consists of multiple thin layers of wood veneer stacked and glued together, while OSB is made of compressed wood strands. Glulam is constructed by layering and gluing together large timber sections, and LVL is made by bonding thin wood veneers together. These products are popular choices for various construction applications.</p></p>
