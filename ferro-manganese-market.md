<p><p>Ferro Manganese is categorized into three types based on carbon content: High Carbon, Medium Carbon, and Low Carbon. High Carbon Ferro Manganese typically contains 70-80% manganese and 6-7% carbon, making it ideal for steel production. Medium Carbon Ferro Manganese ranges from 1.0-2.0% carbon, suitable for specialty steel and foundry applications. Low Carbon Ferro Manganese has a carbon content below 0.5% and is used in stainless steel production due to its low sulfur and phosphorus levels.</p></p>
