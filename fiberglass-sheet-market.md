<p><p>Fiberglass sheets come in two main types: flat and corrugated. Flat fiberglass sheets are smooth, flat panels that are commonly used for applications such as walls, ceilings, and insulation. They provide a clean, modern look and are easy to install. Corrugated fiberglass sheets, on the other hand, have a wavy texture and are commonly used for roofing and siding applications. They offer added strength and durability, making them a popular choice for outdoor structures and buildings. Both types of fiberglass sheets are versatile and offer a range of benefits for various industries.</p></p>
