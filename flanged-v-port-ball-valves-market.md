<p><p>Flanged V Port Ball Valves are commonly available in two main types: Soft Seal Valves and Hard Seal Valves. Soft Seal Valves use a flexible material like PTFE to create a seal, offering better shut-off performance but limited durability. On the other hand, Hard Seal Valves use metal-to-metal contact for sealing, providing enhanced durability and high-pressure capabilities. Users can choose between these options based on their specific application requirements, with each type offering unique benefits in terms of performance and longevity.</p></p>
