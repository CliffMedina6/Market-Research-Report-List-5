<p><p>Fluidized bed dryers are categorized into different types based on their operation and design. Vibrated fluid bed dryers use vibration to enhance fluidization of particles. Fluid-bed granulation involves agglomerating particles in a fluidized bed. Spouted bed dryers use a spout to introduce particles into the fluidized bed. Mechanically agitated fluid-bed dryers have agitators to mix and fluidize particles. Centrifugal fluid-bed dryers use centrifugal force to enhance fluidization. Fluidized spray dryers combine spray drying with fluidized bed technology for efficient drying.</p></p>
