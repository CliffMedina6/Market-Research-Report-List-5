<p><p>The Food and Beverage Industrial Robotics market consists of various types of robots, including Articulated, Cartesian, SCARA, and Delta robots. Articulated robots have multiple joints and mimic the movement of a human arm, making them versatile for a wide range of tasks. Cartesian robots move in a three-dimensional coordinate system, making them precise and efficient. SCARA robots are ideal for fast and repetitive tasks with a horizontal plane of movement. Delta robots are designed for high-speed pick and place applications, providing fast and accurate performance in food and beverage operations.</p></p>
