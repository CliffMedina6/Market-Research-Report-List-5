<p><p>Glass Fiber-reinforced Polymer Composites are used in various markets, including Thermoplastic Plastic Products and Thermosetting Plastic Products. Thermoplastic products can be molded, melted, and reshaped multiple times due to their reversible bonding, making them ideal for recycling. In contrast, Thermosetting products are cured under heat and pressure, resulting in a rigid structure that cannot be reshaped. Both types offer excellent strength-to-weight ratio, corrosion resistance, and durability, making them popular choices in industries such as automotive, aerospace, construction, and marine.</p></p>
