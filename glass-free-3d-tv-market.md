<p><p>The Glass Free 3D TV market is divided into two main types: Active 3D TV and Passive 3D TV. Active 3D TVs use special glasses that rapidly alternate the images seen by each eye, creating a 3D effect. Passive 3D TVs, on the other hand, use polarized glasses to filter different images to each eye, resulting in a more comfortable viewing experience. Both types of TVs offer a unique 3D viewing experience without the need for traditional 3D glasses.</p></p>
