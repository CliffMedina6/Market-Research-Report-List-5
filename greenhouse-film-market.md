<p><p>Greenhouse film market offers three main types of materials: Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (EVA), Linear Low-density Polyethylene (LLDPE), and Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE). EVA films are known for their high transparency and UV resistance, making them ideal for light-sensitive crops. LLDPE films are stronger and more flexible, providing better durability and tear resistance. LDPE films are cost-effective and widely used for shorter growing seasons. Each type of film offers specific advantages depending on the requirements of the greenhouse and the crops being grown.</p></p>
