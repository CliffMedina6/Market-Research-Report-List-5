<p><p>A Hall Current Sensor is a device used to measure the electrical current flowing through a conductor by utilizing the Hall effect. This technology is based on the principle that when a current flows through a conductor, a magnetic field is produced perpendicular to the direction of the current flow. The Hall Current Sensor is able to measure this magnetic field and convert it into a proportional electrical signal, which can then be used to determine the current flow.</p><p>The Hall Current Sensor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.5% during the forecast period.
