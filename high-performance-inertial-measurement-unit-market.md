<p><p>High-Performance Inertial Measurement Unit market types include Mechanical Gyro, Ring Laser Gyro, Fiber Optics Gyro, MEMS, and Others. Mechanical Gyro uses rotating discs to measure angular velocity. Ring Laser Gyro utilizes laser light interference to detect rotation. Fiber Optics Gyro relies on the Sagnac effect in a coiled fiber optic cable. MEMS IMUs use miniaturized sensors on a single chip. Other IMUs may include vibrating gyroscopes or piezoelectric accelerometers. Each type has unique characteristics and applications in sectors such as aerospace, defense, and navigation.</p></p>
