<p><p>High Performance Specialty Elastomer Market offers two main types, Thermoplastic Elastomers (TPE) and Thermosetting Elastomers (TSE). </p><p>TPEs are versatile materials that can be easily molded into various shapes and have properties of both plastics and rubber. They are known for their high elasticity, resilience, and resistance to chemicals and temperature.</p><p>TSEs, on the other hand, are cross-linked polymers that are permanently set in shape during the curing process. They offer excellent heat resistance, mechanical properties, and durability, making them ideal for demanding applications.</p></p>
