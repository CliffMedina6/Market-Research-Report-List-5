AC power cables are used for transmitting electricity in alternating current form, typically for long-distance transmission and distribution of electricity. DC power cables, on the other hand, are used for transmitting electricity in direct current form, commonly used in renewable energy systems and high-voltage direct current (HVDC) transmission lines. Both types of power cables play vital roles in supplying electricity to various industries and urban areas.</p></p>
