<p><p>Hot-pressed NdFeB magnets are available in two main types in the market - isotropic and anisotropic. Isotropic magnets have a uniform magnetic orientation in all directions, making them suitable for applications where magnetic field direction is not critical. On the other hand, anisotropic magnets have a preferred direction of magnetic orientation, providing higher magnetic properties in that direction. Anisotropic magnets are ideal for applications requiring strong and consistent magnetic performance, while isotropic magnets are more versatile but with lower overall magnetic characteristics.</p></p>
