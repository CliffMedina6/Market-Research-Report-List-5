<p><ul><li>Traditional Rice Cookers</li><li>IH (Induction Heating) Rice Cookers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Household mini rice cookers are popular kitchen appliances that come in two main types: Traditional rice cookers and IH (Induction Heating) rice cookers. Traditional rice cookers use heating elements located at the bottom of the cooker to heat up the inner pot and cook the rice evenly. On the other hand, IH rice cookers utilize advanced electromagnetic technology to generate heat directly within the inner pot, resulting in more precise and consistent cooking. Both types cater to different preferences and cooking needs in the market.</p></p>
