<p><p>Indexable cutting tools are commonly categorized into various types based on the material used in their construction. Cemented carbides, also known as tungsten carbides, are widely used due to their hardness and wear resistance. Cermets, which are a combination of ceramics and metals, offer a good balance of toughness and hardness. Ceramics are known for their high-temperature resistance and wear properties. cBN/PcBN tools are made with cubic boron nitride, providing high hardness for machining hardened materials. Diamond tools are the hardest and most abrasive-resistant, ideal for cutting extremely hard materials.</p></p>
