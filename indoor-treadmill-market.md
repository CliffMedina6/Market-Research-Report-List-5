<p><p>Indoor treadmill market can be divided into two main types: Motorised Treadmills and No-motorised Treadmills. Motorised treadmills are equipped with a motor that powers the belt, providing a consistent and reliable running surface. They offer various speed and incline settings, making workouts more customizable. On the other hand, No-motorised treadmills rely on the user's movement to operate the belt, offering a more challenging workout that engages more muscles. Both types cater to different preferences and fitness levels in the market.</p></p>
