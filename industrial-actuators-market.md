<p><p>Industrial actuators market includes automatic electric actuators and drive electric actuators. Automatic electric actuators are self-operating devices that are programmed to perform specific movements or functions without manual intervention. They are commonly used in applications where precise and consistent movement is required. Drive electric actuators, on the other hand, require an external power source to operate and are controlled by an external device such as a motor or hydraulic pump. Both types of actuators play a crucial role in various industries including manufacturing, automotive, and robotics.</p></p>
