<p><p>Industrial circular connectors are categorized based on how they are mounted in a system. Cable mount connectors are attached directly to cables, while flange mount connectors are secured to a panel using a mounting flange. Panel mount connectors are installed directly onto a panel, while PCB mount connectors are soldered onto a printed circuit board. Each type of connector serves a specific purpose and is chosen based on the requirements of the application and the mounting method preferred by the user.</p></p>
