<p><p>Industrial laser drilling equipment market is dominated by two main types - Pulsed Fiber Laser and CW Fiber Laser. Pulsed Fiber Lasers are known for their high peak power and short pulses, making them ideal for applications requiring precision and high speed. On the other hand, CW Fiber Lasers operate continuously, offering high beam quality and stability, making them suitable for drilling deeper holes and achieving higher throughput. Both types cater to different drilling requirements in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
