<p><p>Infrared Diffuse Reflective Photoelectric Sensors are categorized into two main types: Digital Signal and Analog Signal market. Digital Signal sensors provide a binary output signal, indicating the presence or absence of an object within the sensor's range. Analog Signal sensors, on the other hand, provide a continuous output signal that varies based on the distance or reflectivity of the object being detected. Both types of sensors are widely used in industrial automation for applications such as object detection, counting, and positioning.</p></p>
