<p><p>IV securement dressings come in various types such as flat film dressing, dressing with notch, impregnated dressing, and others. Flat film dressings are thin adhesive sheets that provide a barrier against bacteria while allowing for easy monitoring of the IV site. Dressings with notches are designed to accommodate tubing without compromising the integrity of the dressing. Impregnated dressings contain antibacterial agents to prevent infection at the insertion site. Other types of dressings may include foam or gel-based options for added comfort and protection.</p></p>
