<p><p>Laminating pouch films are available in two main types: heat sealed film and cold pouch film. Heat sealed film requires the use of a laminating machine to seal the film around the document, applying heat to create a tight seal. Cold pouch film, on the other hand, does not require heat and instead uses pressure to seal the film around the document. Both types of laminating pouch films offer protection and durability to documents, making them ideal for preserving important papers and photos.</p></p>
