<p><p>Quick Lime and Hydrated Lime are both types of lime used in various industries. Quick Lime, also known as Calcium Oxide, is produced by heating limestone at high temperatures. It is used in construction, agriculture, and water treatment due to its strong alkaline properties. Hydrated Lime, or Calcium Hydroxide, is created by adding water to Quick Lime. It is commonly used in making mortars and plasters, as well as in environmental applications like wastewater treatment. Both types of lime play important roles in different markets due to their unique properties and applications.</p></p>
