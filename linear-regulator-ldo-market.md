<p><p>Linear Regulator (LDO) market can be categorized into Single Channel LDO market and Multi-Channel LDO market. Single Channel LDOs are designed to regulate a single output voltage, making them suitable for applications with only one power rail requirement. On the other hand, Multi-Channel LDOs can regulate multiple output voltages simultaneously, making them ideal for systems that require different power levels for various components. Both types of LDOs offer reliable and efficient voltage regulation solutions for a wide range of electronic devices and systems.</p></p>
