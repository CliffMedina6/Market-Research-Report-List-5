<p><strong>The Liquid Glucose Syrups Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Conversion 20-35 DE</li><li>Intermediate Conversion 35-55 DE</li><li>High Conversion 55-70 DE</li><li>Very High Conversion 70-98 DE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid Glucose Syrups are categorized based on their DE (dextrose equivalent) levels, representing the percentage of reducing sugars. Low conversion syrups (20-35 DE) are less sweet and used in products like dairy and bakery items. Intermediate conversion syrups (35-55 DE) are commonly used in confectionery and beverages. High conversion syrups (55-70 DE) are used for binding and moisture retention in food products. Very high conversion syrups (70-98 DE) are utilized in pharmaceuticals and as a sweetener in various food items.</p></p>
