<p><p>Lithium ion battery active materials market is mainly categorized into four types: cathode materials, anode materials, electrolyte, and others. Electrolytes are crucial for ion movement between cathode and anode. Other materials include separators and binders, which play a supportive role in enhancing the overall efficiency and performance of lithium ion batteries.</p></p>
