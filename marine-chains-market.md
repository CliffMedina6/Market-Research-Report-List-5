<p><ul><li>Stud Link Chain</li><li>Open Link Chain (Short,Mid,Long)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine chains are primarily categorized into three major types: Stud Link Chain, Open Link Chain, and Others. Stud Link Chains are used in heavy-duty applications and are commonly found in offshore drilling rigs and ship anchoring. Open Link Chains, on the other hand, come in three lengths - short, mid, and long - and are used for various maritime purposes such as mooring and towing. The "Others" market consists of specialty chains designed for specific marine applications, including tow chains and buoy chains.</p></p>
