<p><p>Mechanical float level sensors are classified into two main types: Switch Type and Continuous Type. Switch Type sensors provide simple level detection by turning on or off when a certain level is reached. Continuous Type sensors, on the other hand, offer precise and continuous monitoring of liquid levels. While Switch Type sensors are more basic and cost-effective, Continuous Type sensors provide accurate monitoring for applications that require constant level measurements. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
