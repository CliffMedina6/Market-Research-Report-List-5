<p><p>Medical grade heat shrink tubing comes in various types such as Polyolefin, PET, PTFE, and FEP. Polyolefin heat shrink tubing is known for its flexibility and durability, making it suitable for a wide range of medical applications. PET heat shrink tubing offers high clarity and excellent mechanical strength. PTFE heat shrink tubing provides chemical resistance and high temperature tolerance. FEP heat shrink tubing is preferred for its lubricity and non-stick properties. Each type serves specific purposes in the medical industry based on their unique characteristics.</p></p>
