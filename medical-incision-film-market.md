<p><p>Medical incision films are used to protect surgical incisions and wounds from infection and to promote healing. There are various types of medical incision films, including PU (polyurethane) film, PE (polyethylene) film, and iodophor film. PU film is breathable and provides a barrier against bacteria. PE film is transparent and waterproof. Iodophor film contains iodine for antiseptic properties. Each type of film has unique benefits and is chosen based on the specific needs of the patient and the type of surgery performed.</p></p>
