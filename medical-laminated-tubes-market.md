<p><p>Medical laminated tubes are widely used for packaging pharmaceutical and medical products due to their numerous benefits. There are two main types of medical laminated tubes in the market - ABL (aluminum barrier laminated) and PBL (plastic barrier laminated). ABL tubes consist of a layer of aluminum sandwiched between layers of plastic, providing excellent barrier properties against light, moisture, and oxygen. On the other hand, PBL tubes are made entirely of plastic layers, offering a more flexible and cost-effective option for packaging medical products.</p></p>
