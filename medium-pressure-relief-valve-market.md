<p><p>Medium Pressure Relief Valve Market consists of two main types: Spring-Type and Lever-Type valves. Spring-Type valves use a spring mechanism to control the opening and closing of the valve, providing a reliable and consistent pressure relief solution. On the other hand, Lever-Type valves use a lever arm to manually adjust the pressure relief setting, allowing for more flexibility in operation. Both types are widely used in various industries such as oil & gas, chemical, and automotive for maintaining safe pressure levels in equipment and systems.</p></p>
