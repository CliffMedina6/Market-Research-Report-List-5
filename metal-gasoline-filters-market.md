<p><strong>The Metal Gasoline Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-Line Type</li><li>Element/Cartridge Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal gasoline filters market can be classified into two main types: In-Line Type and Element/Cartridge Type. In-Line Type filters are installed directly in the fuel line and are designed for easy maintenance and replacement. Element/Cartridge Type filters contain a replaceable cartridge or element that traps contaminants in the fuel. This type is often used in vehicles with higher fuel flow rates and better filtration efficiency. Both types of filters play a crucial role in ensuring the cleanliness and efficiency of gasoline engines.</p></p>
