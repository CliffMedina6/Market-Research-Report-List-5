<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Testing</li><li>Ultrasonic Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal thickness testers are used for measuring the thickness of metal materials in various industries. There are two main types of metal thickness testers: electromagnetic testing and ultrasonic testing. </p><p>Electromagnetic testing uses electromagnetic induction to measure metal thickness, making it suitable for non-destructive testing of non-ferrous materials. Ultrasonic testing, on the other hand, uses high-frequency sound waves to measure metal thickness, making it ideal for detecting internal defects in metals. Both methods have their own advantages and are widely used in different industries for quality control and inspection purposes.</p></p>
