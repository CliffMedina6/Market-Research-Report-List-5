<p><p>Millimeter-wave substrates are materials used in high-frequency applications where low loss and high thermal stability are important. Types of substrates include LCP (Liquid Crystal Polymer) substrate, PI (Polyimide) substrate, PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) substrate, and others. LCP substrates are flexible and have high thermal stability, making them suitable for high-frequency applications. PI substrates offer high mechanical strength and excellent dielectric properties. PTFE substrates have low dielectric loss and excellent electrical properties. Each type of substrate caters to specific requirements in the millimeter-wave market.</p></p>
