<p><p>Mirror & Effect Pigment Market Types include Natural Mineral Pigments and Artificial Mineral Pigments. Natural mineral pigments are derived from naturally occurring minerals and are considered more environmentally friendly. They offer unique colors and effects but are limited in availability. On the other hand, artificial mineral pigments are synthetically produced to replicate the appearance of natural minerals. They are more readily available and can offer a wider range of colors and effects, but are often considered less environmentally friendly due to the production process.</p></p>
