<p><ul><li>Single Phase</li><li>Three Phase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multi-tariff energy meters are categorized into single phase and three phase markets based on their capacity and functionality. Single phase meters are suitable for residential and small commercial settings, while three phase meters are designed for larger commercial and industrial applications. Single phase meters typically measure energy consumption for a single-phase electrical system, whereas three phase meters are capable of handling three-phase electrical systems. Both types of meters allow for the monitoring and billing of electrical usage based on different tariff rates.</p></p>
