<p><p>The Network on Chip (NoC) market can be broadly classified into two types based on topology: Direct Topology and Indirect Topology. Direct Topology refers to a design where the nodes are directly connected to each other. This type offers low latency and high bandwidth as data can be transferred quickly between nodes. On the other hand, Indirect Topology involves nodes connected through intermediate nodes or switches. This type allows for more flexibility in routing and scalability in larger networks.</p></p>
