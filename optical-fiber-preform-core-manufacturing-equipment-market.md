<p><p>Optical Fiber Preform Core Manufacturing Equipment market consists of two main types: Core Deposition System and Core Sintering System. Core Deposition System is used for depositing core materials onto the inner surface of a glass tube, while Core Sintering System is responsible for fusing the core material with the surrounding cladding material to create a solid preform core. These systems play a crucial role in the manufacturing process of optical fiber preforms, ensuring the quality and performance of the final optical fiber product.</p></p>
