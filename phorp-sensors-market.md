<p><p>PH/ORP Sensors Market includes two main types: Glass Type Sensors and ISFET Sensors. Glass Type Sensors are traditional sensors that use glass electrodes to measure pH and ORP levels in liquids. They are widely used and offer high accuracy and reliability. On the other hand, ISFET Sensors use Ion Sensitive Field Effect Transistors to measure pH levels and offer advantages such as smaller size, faster response time, and lower maintenance requirements. Both types cater to different needs and applications in various industries.</p></p>
