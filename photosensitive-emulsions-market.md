<p><p>Photosensitive emulsions can be categorized into two main types based on their resistance properties: water-resistant and solvent-resistant. Water-resistant photosensitive emulsions are designed to withstand exposure to water without losing their properties or functionality. On the other hand, solvent-resistant emulsions are formulated to maintain their integrity in the presence of various solvents. Both types are widely used in industries such as printing, textiles, and electronics for applications where resistance to either water or solvents is crucial.</p></p>
