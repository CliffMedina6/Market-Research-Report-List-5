<p><p>Polyethylene pellets market is segmented into different types based on their properties and applications. High-density polyethylene (HDPE) pellets are widely used for making bottles, pipes, and containers due to their strength and chemical resistance. Linear low-density polyethylene (LLDPE) pellets are known for their flexibility and puncture resistance, commonly used in packaging films. Low-density polyethylene (LDPE) pellets are softer and more flexible, suitable for products like squeeze bottles and tubing. Other types include metallocene polyethylene and UHMWPE pellets with unique properties for specific applications.</p></p>
