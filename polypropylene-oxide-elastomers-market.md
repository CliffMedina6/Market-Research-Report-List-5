<p><p>Polypropylene oxide elastomers are typically categorized into two main types: mechanical blend type and chemical graft type. Mechanical blend type of elastomers are produced by blending polypropylene oxide with other polymers to improve mechanical properties such as tensile strength and elongation. On the other hand, chemical graft type elastomers are created through chemical reactions that graft functional groups onto the polypropylene oxide backbone, enhancing its chemical and thermal resistance. Both types of elastomers have unique properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
