<p><p>Polypropylene (PP) yarns are commonly divided into two main types: Polypropylene Multifilament Yarn and Polypropylene Monofilament Yarn. </p><p>Polypropylene Multifilament Yarn consists of multiple strands of thin fibers twisted together, resulting in a strong and durable yarn suitable for various applications such as ropes, nets, and textiles.</p><p>On the other hand, Polypropylene Monofilament Yarn is made up of a single thick fiber, providing a smooth and uniform appearance. This type of yarn is often used for applications requiring added flexibility and softness, such as fishing lines and filter fabrics.</p></p>
