<p><p>POM rods are available in two main types, homo-polyoxymethylene and co-polyoxymethylene. Homo-polyoxymethylene rods are made of pure polyoxymethylene material, offering high mechanical strength, stiffness, and low friction properties. On the other hand, co-polyoxymethylene rods are a blend of polyoxymethylene with other materials, providing improved impact resistance and chemical compatibility. Both types of POM rods are widely used in various industries such as automotive, electronics, and consumer goods for applications requiring precise machining and structural stability.</p></p>
