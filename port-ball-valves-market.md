<p><p>Port ball valves come in two main types: soft seal valve and hard seal valve. Soft seal valves are designed with a sealing ring made of soft materials like PTFE, providing efficient leak prevention for low-pressure applications. On the other hand, hard seal valves feature a sealing mechanism made of durable materials like metal, offering superior performance under high-pressure conditions. Both types cater to different industrial needs by providing reliable sealing solutions for various port ball valve applications.</p></p>
