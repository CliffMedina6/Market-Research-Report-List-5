<p><ul><li>High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin</li><li>Low Methoxyl (LMC) Pectin</li><li>Amidated low Methoxyl (LMA) Pectin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Powdered Pectin is mainly classified into three types based on methoxyl content: High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin, Low Methoxyl (LMC) Pectin, and Amidated Low Methoxyl (LMA) Pectin. HM Pectin is used in high sugar content products like jams and jellies, LMC Pectin is used in low sugar or no sugar added products, while LMA Pectin is used in dairy and bakery products. Each type of pectin provides different gelling properties and is suitable for specific food applications.</p></p>
