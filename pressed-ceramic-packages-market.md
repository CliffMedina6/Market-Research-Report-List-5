<p><p>Pressed ceramic packages are used in various markets such as CERTM, GTMS, passivation glass, transponder glass, and reed glass. CERTM packages involve sealing ceramics with metals for electronic applications. GTMS packages use glass and metal sealing for hermetic enclosure of electronic components. Passivation glass packages protect electronic components from environmental factors. Transponder glass packages are used in RFID devices. Reed glass packages are used in sensors and switches. Each market type serves a specific purpose in the electronic industry.</p></p>
