<p><p>Rehabilitation exoskeleton market types include lower body, upper body, and full body exoskeletons. Lower body exoskeletons are designed to assist individuals with lower limb impairments, such as walking disabilities. Upper body exoskeletons focus on supporting arm and shoulder movements, beneficial for patients with upper limb disabilities. Full body exoskeletons offer comprehensive support for both upper and lower limbs, aiding individuals with severe mobility limitations. Each type of exoskeleton is specialized to cater to specific rehabilitation needs and improve overall functionality and movement capabilities.</p></p>
