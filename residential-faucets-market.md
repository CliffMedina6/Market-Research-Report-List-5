<p><p>Residential faucets market can be categorized into three main types: one-hand mixers, pillars, and two-hand mixers. One-hand mixers feature a single handle to control both hot and cold water flow, offering convenience and ease of use. Pillar faucets have separate hot and cold water handles, providing more precise control over water temperature. Two-hand mixers contain two handles for hot and cold water, allowing for customization of water temperature and pressure. Each type caters to different preferences and needs of homeowners.</p></p>
