<p><strong>The Rotational Magnetic Speed Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-contact</li><li>Contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rotational magnetic speed sensors can be categorized into two main types: non-contact and contact sensors. Non-contact sensors use magnetic fields to detect the rotation of a target without physical contact, making them ideal for applications where precision and reliability are crucial. On the other hand, contact sensors rely on direct contact with the rotating target to measure speed. While they may be less costly, they are more prone to wear and tear over time. Both types cater to different industry needs and offer unique benefits.</p></p>
