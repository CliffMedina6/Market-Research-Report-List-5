<p><strong>The Seismic Connector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>V Loop Connector</li><li>U Loop Connector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Seismic connectors are classified into two main types: V Loop Connector and U Loop Connector. V Loop Connectors are designed in a V-shaped configuration and are used to provide flexibility in a structure during seismic events. On the other hand, U Loop Connectors are shaped like a U and are used to prevent the structure from separating during earthquakes. Both types of connectors play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and integrity of buildings in seismic-prone regions.</p></p>
