<p><p>Silicon wafer etching acid market is segmented into three types: hydrofluoric acid, nitric acid, and mixed acid. Hydrofluoric acid is mainly used for silicon dioxide etching, nitric acid is used for silicon etching, and mixed acid is a combination of nitric and hydrofluoric acids used for different etching processes. Each type of acid offers specific benefits and drawbacks, catering to diverse needs in the semiconductor industry. These acids play a crucial role in the fabrication of electronic devices on silicon wafers.</p></p>
