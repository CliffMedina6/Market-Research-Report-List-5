<p><p>Skim Milk Powder (SMP) can be categorized into three main types based on the heat treatment it undergoes during production: Low Heat SMP, Medium Heat SMP, and High Heat SMP. Low Heat SMP is processed at the lowest temperature and retains a lighter color and sweeter flavor. Medium Heat SMP undergoes a slightly higher heat treatment, resulting in a more caramelized flavor and darker color. High Heat SMP is processed at the highest temperature and has a more pronounced flavor and darker color compared to the other two types.</p></p>
