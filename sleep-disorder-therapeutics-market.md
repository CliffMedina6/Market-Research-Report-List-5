Excessive sleep therapeutics target conditions such as narcolepsy or hypersomnia, where individuals experience excessive daytime sleepiness. Disturbed sleep therapeutics are aimed at addressing disruptions in the sleep cycle, such as sleep apnea or restless leg syndrome.
