<p><ul><li>Fixed Type</li><li>Guided Type</li><li>Free Sliding Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spherical bridge bearings are classified into three types based on their functionality: Fixed Type, Guided Type, and Free Sliding Type. Fixed Type bearings are stationary and do not allow any movement. Guided Type bearings permit movement in one direction while restricting movement in other directions. Free Sliding Type bearings allow movement in all directions, providing flexibility to accommodate various bridge loads and movements. Each type is designed to cater to specific requirements and conditions of bridge structures for optimal performance and durability.</p></p>
