<p><p>Structural fumigation services can be categorized into three main market types based on the form in which the fumigant is delivered: solid, liquid, and gas. Solid fumigation involves the use of solid fumigants that slowly release gas when exposed to air. Liquid fumigation utilizes liquid fumigants that evaporate into gas after application. Gas fumigation involves the direct use of gaseous fumigants. Each market type has its own advantages and limitations, making it essential for fumigation companies to choose the most suitable option for specific structural fumigation projects.</p></p>
