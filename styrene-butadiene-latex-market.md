<p><p>Styrene Butadiene Latex market can be divided into two main types: Emulsion Styrene Butadiene Latex and Solution Styrene Butadiene Latex. Emulsion SB Latex is a stable dispersion of polymer particles in water, widely used in paints, adhesives, and coatings. On the other hand, Solution SB Latex is a solvent-based polymer solution, primarily used in specialized applications like sealants and caulks. Both types of SB Latex offer excellent adhesive properties, chemical resistance, and durability, making them popular choices in various industries.</p></p>
