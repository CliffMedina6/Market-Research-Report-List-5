<p><p>Sunshade window film market includes three main types: neutral window film, double reflective window film, and spectral window film. Neutral window film is designed to provide heat and glare reduction while maintaining a natural view. Double reflective window film offers both daytime privacy and heat rejection by reflecting more light than it transmits. Spectral window film selectively filters out specific wavelengths of light to control heat and glare. Each type caters to different needs and preferences, making them popular choices in the market for sunshade window films.</p></p>
