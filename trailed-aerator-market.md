<p><p>Trailed aerators can be categorized into two main types based on their axis orientation: vertical axis and horizontal axis. Vertical axis trailed aerators have tines that rotate in a vertical direction, penetrating the soil vertically. Horizontal axis trailed aerators, on the other hand, have tines that rotate in a horizontal direction, allowing for better aeration of the soil surface. Both types of aerators are commonly used in agriculture and landscaping to improve soil aeration and promote healthy plant growth.</p></p>
