<p><p>Threaded fasteners in the truck fastener market refer to bolts, screws, and nuts that create a secure connection by screwing into a threaded hole. These fasteners are commonly used in truck assembly and repair. On the other hand, non-threaded fasteners include rivets, pins, and clips that provide a secure connection without the need for threading. Both types of fasteners play a crucial role in maintaining the structural integrity and safety of trucks on the road.</p></p>
