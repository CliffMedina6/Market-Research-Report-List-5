<p><strong>The UAV ESCs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>BEC ESCs</li><li>OPTO ESCs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UAV ESCs can be divided into two main market types: BEC ESCs and OPTO ESCs. BEC ESCs come equipped with a Battery Eliminator Circuit which provides power to the receiver and servos, eliminating the need for a separate battery source. On the other hand, OPTO ESCs do not have a BEC circuit and require a separate power source for the receiver and servos. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of UAV owners, offering versatility and flexibility in powering their devices.</p></p>
