Digital I/O modules are used for on/off control, while analog I/O modules are used for continuous signal control. Digital modules are suitable for simple applications where discrete signals are sufficient, while analog modules are essential for applications requiring precise control of variables such as temperature, pressure, or flow. Both types play a crucial role in automation and industrial processes, providing a seamless interface between the control system and external devices.</p></p>
