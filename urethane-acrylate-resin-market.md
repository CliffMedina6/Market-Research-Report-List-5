<p><p>Urethane acrylate resin is classified into two main types based on viscosity: Low viscosity and high viscosity. Low viscosity urethane acrylate resins are more fluid and easier to work with, making them suitable for applications where flow and leveling are important. On the other hand, high viscosity urethane acrylate resins are thicker and typically used in applications where high build and gap-filling properties are required. Both types offer unique characteristics and can be tailored to suit specific project requirements.</p></p>
