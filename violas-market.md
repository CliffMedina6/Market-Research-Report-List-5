<p><p>Violas are classified into two main market types: Acoustic Violas and Electric Violas. Acoustic violas produce sound through the vibration of their strings, creating a traditional warm and rich tone. They are commonly used in classical and orchestral settings. On the other hand, Electric violas utilize electronic pickups to amplify the sound, allowing for greater versatility in tone and volume. They are often used in contemporary music genres and for recording purposes. Both market types cater to different musical preferences and performance needs.</p></p>
